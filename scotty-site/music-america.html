DEATH TO SHOEGAZE (2024)

Created in the last week before I moved away from Boston to New York.
Recorded in the Berklee Practice room and in Colin’s apartment by Fenway.
It was also Alice and Charlie’s apartment.
But we worked on it in Colin’s room.
I like this album. I like how it sounds,
We did it on Colin’s Tascam Portastudio 424 MKiii.
We had a lot of fun making it.
We made a music video for hit single “We Fucked”.
