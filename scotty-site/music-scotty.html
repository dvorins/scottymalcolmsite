Scotty Malcolm is Scotty Malcolm’s personal name and is one of his many musical projects.

ARTHUR FLOWERS: ELF ROCK (2021)

Scotty’s debut album. Created in his hippie era. Some of the songs were written alongside Melody Trautner.

“4.0/5” - RateYourMusic.com

Included in a list entitled “101 DIY Albums you must hear before you die”. ‘Juder’ says, “Scotty is a visionary… indie pop to heal the soul and clear the mind… fantasy and reality... intertwining. Listen while munching on leaves.”

A music video was made for this album for the single, “Mirror Man”. Watch the video. I was 19, I have changed since then. I am now 23. A lot has changed in 4 years. Nino Aiyer played drums on most songs. Eoin played bass on maybe 1 or 2 songs. Mogi Taylor played sax on 2 songs I think. Album mixed and mastered by Justin Kao.

THE SECRET COLOR (2021)

This was a computer album also. Created in Nashville with Rick. We made it in 2 weeks I think. It was really fun to make.

The first time I drank coffee was when we made this album and I immediately fell in love with coffee. I loved coffee for years but now it makes my body feel weird so I drink tea.

Cover art created by the prolific and evasive artist Nico Hagg. Album mixed and mastered by Justin Kao.

DOUBLE ALBUM EXTRAVAGANZA (2023)

This was a compilation tape released by the label HappenTwice. It combined my albums Arthur Flowers: Elf Rock and A Crazy: Named Man. I don’t know how many were created, maybe 25 or 50? I think they are all gone now.

RAT BASTARD (2023)

Just had some songs I made that year. I was busy attending college and becoming smarter. Thought I’d release these songs because I hadn’t released anything for a year. Loosely based on the idea of my alter ego, “Rat Bastard,” who is evil and hates me and takes over my brain and sabotages my life. No one really listened to this one, I didn’t promote it much. It’s okay.

THE PLANET OF THE GREAT SCOTTY (2024)

Compilation of songs I created in the year 2024. Released on cassette tape and on the internet on YouTube. Includes songs recorded on: Logic, Ableton, 4 track cassette, Sounds pretty good.

CORN: TO THE FIRE AND FLAMES (2024)

Recorded at the end of December on 4 track cassette. Made after moving to Brooklyn. Breakup album. Maybe my best album so far. Emo, regrets, mistakes. Released on Bandcamp on DENIZEN RECORDS.
